What is Wholesale Voice?

High-Volume Voice Services for Telecommunications Industry

Businesses in the telecommunications industry use wholesale voice to provide international, long distance, toll free, DID, along with local voice services, as well as additional applications like dial-up, fax, and video conferencing.

Often known as wholesale VOIP, wholesale voice is available in a variety of speeds, ranging from ISDN PRI T1 to OCx. Wholesale VoIP is regarded as a vital component of the international communications industry.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What protocols and codecs do you support?

We support SIP and H.323 protocols along with all major codecs including G.729, G.723, G.711, and GSM. This ensures maximum compatibility with your existing infrastructure and equipment.

What is the minimum payment to get started?

Our wholesale voice termination services require a minimum payment of $500. This gives you immediate access to our full route portfolio, reseller panel, and 24/7 technical support.

What is the difference between Gold and Silver routes?

Gold Routes provide direct connections to Tier 1 carriers with the highest ASR/ACD and intelligent routing - ideal for premium traffic. Silver Routes offer cost-effective rates for local, national, and international calls, perfect for high-volume, budget-conscious operations.

How quickly can I get started?

Once your application is approved and minimum deposit received, your account can be activated within 24 hours. Our team will assist with route configuration and testing to ensure a smooth launch.

Do you provide a reseller panel?

Yes, all wholesale voice clients get access to our full-featured reseller panel. You can monitor real-time call flow, track minutes, view detailed CDRs, manage sub-accounts, and handle instant balance renewals.
