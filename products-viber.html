About The API

What is Viber Business API?

Viber Business API is an interface that allows a business to communicate directly with its audience through Viber. Businesses are able to personally interact with customers and keep them engaged to drive performance and encourage specific actions. By paying a monthly fee and messaging fees, anyone can interact with their clients and customers through this powerful platform.
