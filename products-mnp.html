Understanding MNP

What is Mobile Number Portability?

Mobile Number Portability (MNP) comprises a series of technologies, agreements and a protocol that allows network users to switch between carriers while retaining their mobile phone numbers.

Seamless Carrier Switching

Users can change service providers without changing their phone number

Behind-the-Scenes Complexity

Telecom providers, SMS aggregators, and payment companies need accurate MNO identification

MNP Lookup Solution

Our service provides accurate carrier identification despite number portability
