What Are DID Numbers?

Local Telephone Numbers Without Borders

DID (Direct Inward Dialling) Numbers are local telephone numbers that consist of a large voice circuit or multiple circuits.

  • Route inbound calls directly to particular contacts within your company
  • Eliminate the need for extra physical phone lines
  • No need for callers to navigate through menu options
  • Compatible with voice, fax, voicemail, and instant messaging
Business Benefits

Why Does Your Business Need a DID Number?

Multi-Purpose Compatibility

DID numbers work seamlessly for live direct voice connections, fax, voicemail, and instant messaging. Current technology has made it suitable for all your communication needs.

Direct Connection

Callers can be connected to the appropriate contact automatically without an intermediary, improving customer experience and reducing wait times.

Global Local Presence

Enable customers to contact your business at local call rates, providing a local presence and increasing accessibility. Have a US or UK number routed to your mobile, IP phone, or softphone.

Cost Reduction

Companies with multiple international locations can greatly cut costs by making use of DID numbers instead of maintaining separate physical lines in each location.
