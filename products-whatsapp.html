About The API

What is WhatsApp Business API?

WhatsApp, the well-acclaimed instant messaging platform, has taken a leap into the business world with WhatsApp for Business. The API (Application Programming Interface) allows the messenger to be integrated with chatbots and human service tools, helping large companies connect with customers through instant notifications, personalized services, surveys, and automated responses—all on a dynamic and easy-to-use platform. This is an evolving service taking its roots for a bigger tomorrow.
